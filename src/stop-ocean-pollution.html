Stop Ghost Gear - the most deadly form of marine plastic debris



There is a silent predator in our oceans: abandoned, lost or discarded fishing gear, known as ghost gear. This type of waste can scour the ocean bottom, trap and drown marine wildlife and endanger navigation and fisher economies. Globally, 66% of marine mammals, 50% of seabirds and all sea turtles species have been impacted by marine debris, including ghost gear.

Let’s stop plastics choking our oceans – and harming people and nature! We urgently need a global legally binding agreement that involves every country in ending this crisis.

We are calling the goverments, fishing gear designers and producers, fishers and the public to tackle the problem of ghost gear and stop our oceans drowing in plastic
