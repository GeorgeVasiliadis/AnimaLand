The Organization

We are a non-profit organization of young people, sensitive about our environment. The AnimaLand project was initially developed by Computer Science students at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. We aim to help our classmates, colleagues and fellow citizens learn about the factors that threaten our planet and the animals on it. We also strive to motivate more people to join our eco-community and take action!
