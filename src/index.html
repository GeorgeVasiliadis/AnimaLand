A Lifeful Forest

Broiler Chickens

A Beautiful Landscape

Research showed that 10 minutes in a natural setting can help college students feel happier and lessen the effects of both physical and mental stress.

Some Peaceful Mountains

About 80% of our planet's fresh water originates in the mountains.

Dried Land

Dry lands cover 65% of the African continent.
